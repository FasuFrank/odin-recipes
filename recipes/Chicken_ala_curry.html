Chicken Curry

If you are of the peopel who likes the spicy things, you have to make sure to have from time to time a little time to taste this Chicken Recipe. The taste given by the indian spices makes everyone want to cry (both metaphoricaly and literally)

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of Chicken
  • Curry powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Coconut Milk

Steps:

  1. In a crockpot, bring all the ingredients and set the fire to low
  2. You must let all cook for a good time, so be patient, the result will worth the time
  3. Once all the ingredients inside the pot look well blended just by the action of the heat,it is time to turn off the fire
  4. Now serve with a rice bed and embrace yourself for the spice!!!
