Eggplant Lasagna

An alternative for the people who wants to avoid the consume of pasta due to the high content of carbohydrates and for those who can't eat gluten. This Eggplant lasagna will be the best dish you could eat and at the same time take care of your figure

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggplants
  • Cheese
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 500 gr grounded beef
  • 2 Onions

Steps:

  1. Slice the eggplants to make the lasagna layers
  2. Cook up your grounded beef with the spices of your choice
  3. Make a hefty tomato sauce letting the tomatoes cook for a while
  4. Alternate between adding a layer of eggplant, a layer o ground beef and a layer of tomato sauce
  5. Once you have finished, sprinkle the top with enough cheese according to your liking
  6. Enjoy of your Lasagna!!
